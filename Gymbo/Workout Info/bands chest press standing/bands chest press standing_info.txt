1. Stand with feet should width apart. Grasp the handles with palms facing down, with the band portion behind you.
2. Bring you arms up with your elbows high and fists point forward.
3. Keeping your elbows and wrist in line with your shoulders, press out and contract. Do not lock your elbows.
4. Hold for 1-2 seconds, then release back to the starting position.
