1. Lie on your back and place the resistance band under your upper back, preferably underneath your should blades.
2. Grasp the end of the handles with each hand. With your shoulder to elbow flat on the ground and your hands facing straight up.
3. At the same time, press your arms straight up to the ceiling. Bring your hands together to form a triangle motion.
4. Hold and contract for approx. 2-3 seconds, then lower back slowly to the starting position.
