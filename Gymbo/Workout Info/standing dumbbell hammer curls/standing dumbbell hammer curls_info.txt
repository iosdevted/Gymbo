1. Stand with feet shoulder width apart and knees slightly bent. You can either do this with one arm at a time, or both.
2. Hold dumbbells in both hands with the palms of your hands facing your body.
3. Keeping the elbows stationary at your side, curl the dumbbell up. Keep the palm facing in the same direction as before, do not twist it at the top.
4. Hold for 2 seconds, contracting the side of your bicep.
5. Slowly return to the starting position.
