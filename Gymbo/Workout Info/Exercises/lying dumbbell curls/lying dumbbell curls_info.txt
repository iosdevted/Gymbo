1. This movement is performed with dumbbells. It is performed lying on a flat bench.
2. Lie on a bench with your feet flat on the ground. Let your hands hang straight down holding a dumbbell each with palms facing up.
3. Begin with the dumbbells extended down, touching the ground. Now, one arm at a time, slowly curl up the weight. Be sure to keep your elbow pointing down as you come up, you may raise it slightly to contract your biceps.
4. Hold and contract for 1-2 seconds, then release back down. Repeat with opposite arm.
