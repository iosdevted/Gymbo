1. Begin the movement by sitting on the machine and aligning the seat if possible to align the handles with your upper chest.
2. Grasp the bar or handles so they are shoulder or slightly higher level to you. Your palms are facing out. Be sure to keep your back straight.
3. Press the bar or weight straight up. Be sure not to lock your elbows at the top.
4. Hold for 1-2 seconds then lower slowly for to the starting position.
