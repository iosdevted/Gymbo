1. Please begin this exercise in a standard push up position. With your back flat, support yourself by your feet and hands firmly on the ground below you. Palms facing forward.
2. Your body in a fully extended position, exhale and begin to raise your buttocks straight up while keep hands and feet firmly in their place.
3. Continue until you form a v-position. with your arms and torso fully extended and your head aligned with your spine tucked between your arms,
4. Hold, then inhale as you begin to lower back to the starting position.
