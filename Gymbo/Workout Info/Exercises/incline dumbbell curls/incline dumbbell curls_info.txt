1. Sit on incline bench with head resting on pad or slightly raised. Hold the dumbbells with palms facing each other and extended down towards the floor.
2. One arm at a time, curl the weight up keeping your elbow close to your side. At the top of the movement, twist your wrist for your palms to face you.
3. Contract and hold for 1-2 seconds, then release back to the extended starting position. Repeat with opposite arm.
