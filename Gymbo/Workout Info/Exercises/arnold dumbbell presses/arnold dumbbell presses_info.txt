1. You may be in a seated or standing position during this exercise. Grab a dumbbell in each hand and bring the weights curled up directly in front of your shoulders with your elbows at your sides. Be sure that your palms are turned in towards you.
2. Slowly press the dumbbells directly above your shoulders while turning your palms out as your reach the top of the movement.
3. Press the dumbbells to the point where your elbows almost lock out.
4. Lower the weights in the reverse manner, ending the movement with your palms facing in towards you and the weights steady by your shoulders.
