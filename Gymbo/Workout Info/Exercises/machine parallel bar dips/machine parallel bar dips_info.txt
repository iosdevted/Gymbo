1. This movement is performed with a parallel dips structure or machine.
2. Grasp a handle with each hand, overhand grip. Raise and hold yourself at arms length.
3. Next, lower yourself down, bending at the elbows. Lower yourself to the point where you feel a stretch in your chest muscles, approximately where your arms form a 90 degree angle. Be sure to lean forward slightly. Hold for 1-2 seconds.
4. Raise to your starting position.
