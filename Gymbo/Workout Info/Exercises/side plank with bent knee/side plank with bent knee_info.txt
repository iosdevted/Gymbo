1. Begin by laying on either said, let's start with your right. Support your upper body by leaning on your right elbow in a comfortable and supportive position. Rest your legs and hips on the floor with your legs in a bent position.
2. With your left arms resting on your left thigh, slowly raise your hip off the floor until you form a straight line from your head to your knees. Your knees will remain on the floor.
3. Hold and lower back slowly. Repeat with other side.
