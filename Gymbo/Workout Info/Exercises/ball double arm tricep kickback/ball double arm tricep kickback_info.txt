1. Lay with your chest on the ball, knees on the floor. Grab dumbbells in both hands.
2. Hold the dumbbells with your palms facing the ball. Keep your upper arms parallel to the ground.
3. Slowly extend your arms behind you, keeping your elbows stationary.
4. Hold for 1 second, contracting the triceps muscles.
5. Slowly return to the starting position.
