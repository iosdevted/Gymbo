1. This movement is performed on a flat bench. Locate this bench between two ground level cables. Lay down on the bench, placing your feet flat on the ground.
2. Be sure to keep your back flat against the bench and your feet flat on the ground. Grasp a cable handle in each hand. Lie flat with your arms extended out to each side.
3. Simultaneously, bring each cable directly above your chest, in an arc range of motion. Be sure to keep your elbows slightly bent during all times.
4. Contract and hold for 1-2 seconds, then lower your arms back down in the same arc.
