1. This movement is performed with a dumbbell in a seated position.
2. Sit on a bench, leaving enough room to place your arms on. Bend forward slightly.
3. Grasp the dumbbell with an underhand grip. Your forearm should be on the bench, with your wrist over the edge of the bench. Curl the dumbbell upwards, contracting the inner forearm muscles.
4. Slowly lower the weight back to a fully extended position. Repeat the movement.
