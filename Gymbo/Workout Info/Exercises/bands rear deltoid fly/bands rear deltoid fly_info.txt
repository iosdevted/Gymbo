1. Stand with feet flat on the ground on top of the middle of the resistance band. With knees slightly bent, lean over bending at your hips. Keep your head up and back flat.
2. Grasp a handle in each arm and make an "X" as is shown in the photo above.
3. Keeping your back flat, pull out to each side with each arm at the same time.
4. Hold at the top and contract for 1-2 seconds. Release and return to starting position.
