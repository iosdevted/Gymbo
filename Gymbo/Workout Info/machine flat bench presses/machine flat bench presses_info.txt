1. This movement is performed on a flat chest press machine. When you are in position, place your feet flat on the ground for balance and to avoid injury to lower back.
2. Grasp the handles or bar with a wider-than-shoulder-width grip.
3. Press the handles or machine or bar directly forward or in the path the machine allows you.
4. Hold and contract for 1-2 seconds. Then release back to the starting position.
