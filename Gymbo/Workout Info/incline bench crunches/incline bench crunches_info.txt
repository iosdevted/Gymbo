1. This exercise is performed on an inclined bench with leg supports at the top.
2. Sit on the bench and hook your legs up to the supports.
3. Lean back and stretch the abdominals. Place your arms across your chest.
4. Raise your body towards your knees, keeping your backside on the bench. Contract the abdominal muscles.
5. Hold for 2 seconds, then slowly go back down, but not touching the bench with your shoulders in order to keep your abdominals flexed. Repeat.
