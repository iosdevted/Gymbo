1. This movement may be performed in a seated or standing position. Please be sure to rotate the variations for greater benefit.
2. Either sitting or standing, grasp a dumbbell in each hand and bend forward from your waist. Palms facing in. Bring the dumbbells together in front of you, keeping your elbows slightly bent.
3. Now keeping a strict form, raise the dumbbells to either side, be sure to keep your palms facing down.
4. Hold for 1-2 seconds and slowly release the weight back to the starting position.
