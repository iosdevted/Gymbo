1. You will be using a Smith machine. Place a seat preferably with back supports below the path of the bar.
2. Grasp the bar with an over hand grip. Lower the bar to your upper chest area, or to the limit your range of motion will allow to that point.
3. Press the bar up, without locking your elbows.
4. Hold for 1-2 seconds, then release back down to the starting position.
