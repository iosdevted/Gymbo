1. Lean on a stability ball with your feet flat on the ground. Create a slight incline angle with your body.
2. Grasp a dumbbell in each hand, with palms facing out / forward. Arms extended.
3. One arm at a time, curl the dumbbell up keeping your elbow close to your side.
4. Hold and contract for 1-2 seconds, then release back down. Repeat with the opposite arm.
