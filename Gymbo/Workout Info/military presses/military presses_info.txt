1. This movement should be performed in a seated position, preferably with back support. Grasp a barbell and raise it to your upper chest. Use an underhand grip, supporting the bar from underneath. Your grip should be shoulder width.
2. Press the barbell straight up, stopping right before your elbows would lock out.
3. Hold for 1-2 seconds, then release back to the starting position on the upper part of your chest.
