1. Seated on a stability ball, with back straight and feet flat on the floor in front of you.
2. Grasp a dumbbell in each hand with palms facing each other. Hands fully extended at your sides.
3. One arm at a time, curl the weight up keeping your elbow close at your side. Twist your wrist at the top of the movement until your palms are facing you.
4. Hold and contract for 1-2 seconds, then lower and repeat with opposite arm.
