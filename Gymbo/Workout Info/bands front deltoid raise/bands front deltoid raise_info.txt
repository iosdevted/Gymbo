1. Stand with your feet shoulder width apart. keep your knees slightly bent.
2. Holding the resistance band handle in one hand, place on foot on the band so that there is no slack with your arm hanging down.
3. Keep your body still, raise your arm straight in front of you while holding the handle. Raise your arm to should height or eye-level.
4. Hold at the top of the movement for 1-2 seconds, then slowly lower your arm back to the starting position.
5. After a set, perform the movement with your other arm.
