1. This movement is performed with a leg press machine which can either be flat or inclined to the ground.
2. Sit on the padded seat. Place one leg in front of you, not on the pad. Place the other leg on the pad as shown in the first picture.
3. Unlock the weight and push the weight up through the heel of your foot, contracting the thigh muscles.
4. Slowly return the weight to the starting position and repeat.
5. Repeat with the other leg.
