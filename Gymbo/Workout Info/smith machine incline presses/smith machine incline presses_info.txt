1. This movement is performed on a slightly inclined machine bench, roughly 30 degrees. Lay down on the bench, placing your feet flat on the ground. This is performed on a smith machine.
2. Be sure to keep your back flat against the bench. Grasp the bar or handles on the machine with a wide grip, palms facing forward and your elbows straight down.
3. Press the bar or machine up in the groove provided.
4. Hold and contract for 1-2 seconds, then release back down to the starting position.
