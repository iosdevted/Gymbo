1. Lay on a bench. Feet flat on the ground.
2. Grab hold of a barbell, use am E-Z curl bar if possible. Grasp the barbell with an overhand grip, with your thumbs on the outside. Be sure to lock your wrist during the exercise.
3. Keeping your elbows close to pointing straight up and shoulder-width apart, press the wieght stright up until your arms are fully extended.
4. Hold for 2 seconds, contracting the triceps muscles.
5. Slowly bring the weight towards your forehead, keeping your elbows stationary. Slowly return to the extended position.
