1. This movement is performed on a flat bench. When you are in position, place your feet flat on the ground for balance and to avoid injury to your lower back.
2. Grasp the bar with a wider-than-shoulder-width grip. The grip should be so when you lower the weight to your chest, your forearms should point straight up.
3. Raise the bar directly above your chest. Next, slowly lower the bar to your lower chest area.
4. Hold for 1 second and then raise bar back up to the top. Hold for 2 seconds.
