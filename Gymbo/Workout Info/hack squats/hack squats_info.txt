1. Lay back-rest with the pads on your shoulders and your feet shoulder width apart on the platform.
2. Push off the platform using your heels and keeping you back flat against the support, then unlock the weight. Do not lock your knees out.
3. Slowly lower the weight by bending at the knees until your knees are 90 degrees.
4. By pushing with the heels of your feet, slowly return to the standing position, contracting your thighs.
