1. This movement is performed on a slightly inclined bench, roughly 30 degrees. Lay down on the bench, placing your feet flat on the ground.
2. Be sure to keep your back flat against the bench. Grasp the barbell with a wide overhand grip.
3. Lift the bar from its holders or rack and hold it directly above. Slowly lower the bar to your upper chest area.
4. Hold for 1 second then raise bar straight back up, hold there for 2 seconds.
